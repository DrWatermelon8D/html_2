Sloppy Joe

Description:

A Sloppy Joe is a sandwich consisting of ground beef and onions in a tomato-based sauce served on a hamburger bun.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pound ground beef
  • 1 Medium onion
  • 1 Medium green pepper
  • Hamburger buns

Steps:

  1. Dice Half of the onion, and the whole Green Pepper.
  2. Cook beef, onion, and green pepper together.
  3. Drain the liquid from the meat and veggies.
  4. Add salt pepper and other seasonings to taste.
